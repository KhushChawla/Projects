1. Geo-Spatial PM2.5 Trend Analysis using GIS and Data Analytics [India Meteorological Department (IMD)]

This project involved analyzing seven years (2018–2024) of PM2.5 data collected from multiple Indian cities. Pre-processed and categorized the data into seasonal and annual datasets, performed statistical trend analysis, and created GIS-based thematic maps using QGIS. The objective was to identify long-term spatial and temporal pollution patterns to support air quality monitoring and environmental forecasting.

2. GIS-Based Solar Suitability Analysis for Maharashtra [Delhi Technological University (DTU)]

The objective of this project was to identify the most suitable locations for solar power generation across Maharashtra using GIS. Integrated multiple spatial datasets, including Global Horizontal Irradiance (GHI), cloud cover, temperature, and land-use/land-cover, and applied multi-criteria spatial analysis in QGIS to generate solar suitability maps for supporting renewable energy planning.

3. Environmental Impact Assessment and Biodiversity Analysis of DTU Campus [Delhi Technological University (DTU)]

This project was carried out as part of an Environmental Impact Assessment for the proposed construction of Academic Block-1 at DTU. Conducted ecological field surveys, tree inventory, biodiversity assessment using diversity indices, and green cover loss analysis. Based on the findings, mitigation measures and compensatory plantation strategies were proposed to support sustainable infrastructure development.

4. Energy Audit and Sustainable Lighting Optimization of Academic Blocks AB-3 & AB-4 [Delhi Technological University (DTU)]

This project focused on evaluating electricity consumption in the corridor lighting systems of DTU's Academic Blocks AB-3 and AB-4. Analyzed energy usage, operational costs, carbon emissions, and equipment lifespan, followed by a cost-benefit analysis of various solutions. A sensor-based automated lighting system was proposed to improve energy efficiency, reduce operational costs, and lower carbon emissions.

5. Environmental Regulatory Workflow and Decision Support Analysis [Oil and Natural Gas Corporation Limited (ONGC)]

This project involved studying the PARIVESH 2.0 portal and India's environmental regulatory framework. Analyzed digital workflows for Environmental, Forest, Wildlife, and Coastal Regulation Zone (CRZ) clearances, examined the Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) process, and evaluated how digital decision-support systems improve transparency, regulatory compliance, and environmental governance.
